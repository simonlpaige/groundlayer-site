// About

Groundlayer LLC is a Kansas City technology company founded on a simple question: who benefits when machines do most of the work? We believe the answer should be everyone. Our projects span web development, SaaS, community tech literacy, and economic systems design - all pointed at the same goal: replacing costly necessities with automated, community-owned services.
